# Summative Coding Four App Development Studio
UAL CCI BSc Creative Computing, Year 2, 2026

Flock Together App for bird watching collective in London (and the diaspora). Tackles the issue of Flock being a seasonal community initiative (as it is an outdoor, walking club) tied to the warmer months. Also communication is spread out across multiple platforms: eventbrite, whatsapp, instagram, email & website. This app is a digital design solution that centralises all information and communication, as well as making the community accessible all year around. Thus by creating an app, the community is served, strengthened and better connected.
